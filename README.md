# RBLauncher

A lightweight Python-based Minecraft launcher.

## Requirements

* Python 3.7+
* macOS
* Java 21+

## How to Use

1. Download the zip from the latest release [here](https://github.com/braydenwatt/A-Really-Bad-Fabric-Minecraft-Launcher/releases/latest).
> [!IMPORTANT]
> For version 2.0.3+ simply download the Source code (zip).
<img width="1197" height="785" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-20 at 12 31 59 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a1f13b6b-1a4e-4cbe-a7f7-3fa945331cc5" />

2. Download "Java 21 JDK" from Self Service.
<img width="800" height="648" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-20 at 12 22 59 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c7ac74c1-a8b5-4589-8810-590e8b1e60cd" />

3. Extract the zip and right click on the folder. Press "New Terminal at Folder."
<img width="800" height="648" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-20 at 12 29 06 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b363c5e9-84ca-467d-85e3-d045e1e46c68" />

4. In the terminal that appears type "xattr -rc launcher.command," press Enter.
<img width="598" height="370" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-20 at 12 29 23 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4039d888-fea3-4358-a0f4-458f1c3ffeda" />

5. Double click launcher.command inside the folder.
<img width="598" height="370" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-20 at 12 29 31 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9e8f29f3-e783-42b9-a604-939dbf75dfc0" />

6. Click on "Username" in the bottom left.
<img width="1197" height="785" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-20 at 12 30 02 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6e29911f-8eed-4091-9377-0d11c2044d53" />

7. Click "Log In."
<img width="1197" height="785" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-20 at 12 30 07 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/462b289a-97ec-4454-8e7c-743cfc8b07a3" />

8. Press "Continue with Microsoft," a dialog will appear and you will be redirect to either a Microsoft sign-in page or redirection page. After reaching "You have reached a page that is not externally shown..." copy the full url including code=... Ignore the warning, this is the only way I could make it work. After copying the url, past it back into the dialog.
<img width="1197" height="785" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-20 at 12 30 34 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/efb43c9b-27e1-45ac-8751-8d4c6764ab1d" />

9. Thats it!

10. You can add a Minecraft instance by pressing "New Instance" in the top right. The menu will walkthrough the rest.
<img width="1197" height="785" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-20 at 12 31 08 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/64d6052c-882d-4b2c-aa9d-dace8a95b5bd" />

If you encounter any bugs or issues you can lmk.
